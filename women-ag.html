📋 How Mentorship Works
1. Complete Your Profile
Tell us your farm type, goals, and what you most want to learn from a mentor.
2. Get Matched
Our AI matches you with women farmers by province, farm type, and goals. Most matches are within 1 province.
3. First Call in 7 Days
30-minute video call via Zoom or phone. No pressure — just conversation between farmers.
4. Ongoing Connection
Monthly check-ins, shared resources, and access to Women Ag peer groups facilitated by your mentor.
